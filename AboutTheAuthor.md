Ben Tubbing was born in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, 1957

Married in 1988, with three daughters

Studies
- Applied Physics at Delft University
- PhD at the University of Utrecht on microwave methods for measuring electron temperature in Nuclear Fusion devices
- Detached to the Joint European Torus (JET) in Culham, UK

1987: Professional
- JET Physics Operations: running experiments on the machine
- Coordinating JET's contributions to the design of ITER (International Thermonuclear Experimental Reactor)

- In 1998 moved to EU Commission, Brussels, Directorate General Research
- Research funding in the area of advanced materials and nanotechnology
- Contributed to setting up the European Research Council

2022: Retirement
- Continued life long hobbies in mechanical engineering, electronics and software
- Designed and made a globally unique parametric excitation "free" pendulum
- Publications in the *Horological Journal*, 2024, 2025, 2026
